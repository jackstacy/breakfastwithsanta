Join Us for

Breakfast With Santa

The Colleyville Lions Club proudly hosts the 28th Annual Breakfast with Santa. This event’s proceeds go to support local and Lion-designated charities and Colleyville Lions Club scholarships.

Dec 2, 2023 at Crown of Life Lutherian Church

Event Details

When and Where

Dec 2, 2023 at Crown of Life Lutheran Church, 6605 Pleasant Run Rd., Colleyville, TX 76034

Santa!

Santa will be on-site for children to get photos with him!

Breakfast

Pancakes & Trimmings will be served for the duration of the event!

Actvities

Christmas Tree Maze, Visit with Dasher, Glitter Painting, Balloons, Family Service Projects, and more!

Donations Welcome!

We ask attendees to bring canned food for GRACE and/or an old pair of eyeglasses, mobile phones for recycling to the visually impaired, or hearing aids to be Distributed by the Lions District 2E2 Eyeglass Recycling Center.

Gift Donations

For those who want their children to experience the giving spirit of the season, please bring an unwrapped gift for a child. There will be a wrapping station for children to wrap their gifts and put them in one of Santa’s Sleighs to be distributed to children by Colleyville Lions and Santa at the GRACE Christmas distribution.

Christmas Cards

Christmas Cards will be provided for those serving in the Military overseas. Children and their parents will be able to add a note to put a smile on the faces of those who cannot be with their families during this holiday season.

Tickets

One ticket pays for your entire family! $10 in advance and $12 at the door

Would Your Organization Like to Become a Sponsor?

If your organization, business, or you would like to sponsor Breakfast with Santa, the Colleyville Lions Club is a 501(c)(3) charity, and all donations are tax-exempt and tax-deductible. Our Sponsor Support is essential for the continued overall success of this Annual Community Event. In addition, for those with businesses in Colleyville, the City of Colleyville is again offering grants to help those companies with visibility in the community.

Download the Sponsorship Form

Candy Cane Sponsor

A $250 donation will receive 10 event tickets, social media exposure, company advertising electronically displayed at the event.

Elf Sponsor

A $500 or more donation will include 20 event tickets, social media exposure, company advertising electronically displayed at the event, 2 times more often than a Candy Cane Sponsor; and special recognition announcements at the event.

Santa’s Helper

- A $1,000 donation will provide the sponsor special recognition to include 40 event tickets, social media exposure, company advertising electronically displayed at the event, 4 times more often than an Elf Sponsor, and special recognition announcements at the event.

Santa’s Partner

A $2,000 and above donation will provide the sponsor special recognition to include 100 event tickets, social media exposure, company advertising electronically displayed at the event, 8 times more often than a Santa’s Helper, and special recognition announcements at the event.
